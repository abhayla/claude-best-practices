🤖 The Robot's Report Card

Don't build for autonomy — prove the trust score first.
We are teaching a robot to do jobs. Before the robot is allowed to do a job ALL BY ITSELF, it has to earn a good report card (a score out of 100). If the score is high AND the robot broke no safety rules, the card says the robot MAY work alone. But right now we are in 'shadow mode': the card is printed, but a grown-up still presses the button every single time. We watch quietly to make sure the report card is honest. Only when it has been honest for a long time do we let the robot act on it. Now the REAL jobs have begun: a second robot named Atlas did 11 of the 30 honest real jobs we need, and so far the card has told the truth on every one of them.
